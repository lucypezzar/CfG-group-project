Chicken Curry

User Rating 3.5/5

Serves:

Prep time:

5 mins

Cook time:

45 mins

Delicious Classic Indian Dish

Super good for lazy days, hectic days and family gatherings

Easy,efficient and effortless

Ingredients

Olive oil
Medium onions
Chicken thighs (boneless)
Garlic Cloves
Chopped Tomatoes
Spice Paste
Greek Yoghurt
Ginger
Coriander

Instructions

  1. Put saucepan on medium heat and add olive oil
  2. Reduce heat and add onions,salt and pepper, ginger and garlic
  3. Chop chicken and fry for 5 minutes
  4. Bring to boil and lower to a simmer and cook on gentle heat for 25-35 minutes
  5. stir the yoghurt, coriander and serve with rice , chapatti or naan
