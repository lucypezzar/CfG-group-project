Spaghetti bolognese

User Rating: 4/5

Serves:

Prep time:

25 mins

Cook time:

1 hour and 50 mins

True Italian Dish- Simple and easy.

Filled with a creamy tomato based chilli sauce

Ingredients

Olive oil
Medium onions
Meat mince (beef or turkey)
Plum Tomatoes
Tomato Puree
Spaghetti
Grated Parmesan
Mixed herbs
Meat stock cube

Instructions

  1. Put saucepan on medium heat and add olive oil
  2. Reduce heat and add onions and the half the mixed herbs
  3. Increase heat and add mince until meat is browned over
  4. Add plum tomato, meat stock cube and the rest of the mixed herbs
  5. Bring to boil and add puree
  6. Add grated parmesan and stir
  7. When bolognese sauce is nearly finished cook spaghetti accordingly
