Sweet and Sour Chicken

User Rating 4.5/5

Serves:

Prep time:

25 mins

Cook time:

30 mins

healthier takeaway from your home! Classic Chinese dish- simple and scrumptious

Ingredients

Pineapple
Medium onions
Chicken breast
Ginger and garlic
Soy and oyster sauce
Dried Chilli Flakes
Cornflour
Sweet and sour sauce
Brown sugar
Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Put saucepan on medium heat and add olive oil
  2. Reduce heat and add onions and diced coated cornflour chicken thighs
  3. Increase heat and add pineapple with sweet and sour sauce
  4. To make the sauce you add black pepper, cornflour, sugar, water and soy and oyster sauce
  5. Add the sauce to the pineapple and chicken mixture
  6. Cook for around 20 mins stirring every 4 minutes
  7. When done serve with sliced spring onions and vegetables
